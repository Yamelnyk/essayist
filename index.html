How It Works

  • Choose Citation Style

    EssayWriter currently supports APA, MLA, Chicago/ Turabian & Harvard. Stay tuned for more citation styles!

  • Add References

    Use EssayWriter’s Reference Manager & built-in Google Scholar support to easily add references at any time.

  • Write & Cite

    Let EssayWriter handle the formatting and begin writing your essay stress-free! Insert citations with the click of a button.

Automatic
Formatting

Let EssayWriter handle the formatting so you can focus on the content of your essay. From page set-up to citations, footnotes & references, EssayWriter takes care of everything.

Reference
Manager

Add and manage references using EssayWriter’s built-in reference manager. Create collections and easily add sources and EssayWriter will generate a perfectly formatted reference list.

Citations

Insert in-text citations or footnotes seamlessly. With a click of a button EssayWriter presents a list of your references. Tap the reference you want to cite and continue writing.

Google Scholar

Search for references in the familiar Google Scholar interface and simply click Add to EssayWriter on any reference you find. Alternatively, use EssayWriter’s built-in book and journal search or simply paste a URL

APA, MLA,
& more

EssayWriter currently supports four commonly-used citation styles: APA, Chicago/Turabian, Harvard and MLA. Stay tuned for many more citation styles!

Various
Reference Types

From Journal Articles, books and websites, to legal cases, datasets and interviews, EssayWriter covers a large variety of different types of references.

Figures & Tables

Easily add figures and tables to your essays, while EssayWriter ensures the correct formatting of titles and captions.

Extra Sections

EssayWriter supports extra sections such as Table of Contents, Abstract, Appendices and Chapters.
