How It Works

  • Choose Citation Style

    Essayist currently supports APA, MLA, Chicago/ Turabian & Harvard. Stay tuned for more citation styles!

  • Add References

    Use Essayist’s Reference Manager & built-in Google Scholar support to easily add references at any time.

  • Write & Cite

    Let Essayist handle the formatting and begin writing your essay stress-free! Insert citations with the click of a button.

Automatic
Formatting

Let Essayist handle the formatting so you can focus on the content of your essay. From page set-up to citations, footnotes & references, Essayist takes care of everything.

Reference
Manager

Add and manage references using Essayist’s built-in reference manager. Create collections and easily add sources and Essayist will generate a perfectly formatted reference list.

Citations

Insert in-text citations or footnotes seamlessly. With a click of a button Essayist presents a list of your references. Tap the reference you want to cite and continue writing.

Google Scholar

Search for references in the familiar Google Scholar interface and simply click Add to Essayist on any reference you find. Alternatively, use Essayist’s built-in book and journal search or simply paste a URL

APA, MLA,
& more

Essayist currently supports four commonly-used citation styles: APA, Chicago/Turabian, Harvard and MLA. Stay tuned for many more citation styles!

Various
Reference Types

From Journal Articles, books and websites, to legal cases, datasets and interviews, Essayist covers a large variety of different types of references.

Figures & Tables

Easily add figures and tables to your essays, while Essayist ensures the correct formatting of titles and captions.

Extra Sections

Essayist supports extra sections such as Table of Contents, Abstract, Appendices and Chapters.

  • What is Essayist?


    Essayist is a specialized word processor with a built-in reference manager. Write, cite, and manage/store references all in one app.

  • Who is Essayist for?


    Essayist was designed for anyone who writes academic papers: from high school and university/college students to researchers and professionals.

  • How does Essayist work?


    Choose your citation style and start writing! Essayist is designed with a simple and intuitive interface and only has features relevant to academic writing.

  • How much does Essayist cost?


    Essayist has two subscription options: $5.99/Month or $39.99/Year ($3.33/Month).

  • What citation styles does Essayist Support?


    Essayist currently APA 7, MLA 8 & 9, Turabian 9 (Chicago), and several Harvard Style variations.

  • Can I generate references in Essayist?


    Yes! Essayist o ff ers many options to add and generate references: Built-in Google Scholar search, built-in book & journal search, paste a URL, manual entry forms, and a custom reference form.

    Essayist is a universal app and can be used across iPhone, iPad, & Mac.

    Essayist requires macOS 11 or later and iOS 15 or later.
